Rules

Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a dice until 1 is rolled or they decide to "hold"

If the player rolls any other number than 1, it is added to their current score and the player's turn continues. If a player chooses to "hold", their current score is added to their total score, and it becomes the next player's turn.

The first player to score 100 or more points wins.
